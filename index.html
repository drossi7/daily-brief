Today's Shoot
Sunday morning — the best light of the week
▼
What to Shoot Today

Sunday morning in Calgary — this is your best shooting window of the week. Slow, unhurried light. Nobody on the streets. The city is quiet. That stillness is the whole mood.

The moment: A window with early spring light — maybe a plant, a book, your coffee. April in Calgary means the light is finally getting warm and directional. Use it.

Lens: 40mm f/2 wide open. Stay close. Let the background fall away. Good window light is all you need.

Tip: Film 3–4 angles of the same moment. You'll only use 20 seconds — but you want options in the edit.
