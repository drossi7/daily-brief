Today's Shoot
What to Shoot Today

Today you introduce the whole concept. Show your morning — coffee, quiet light, your camera on the table — and end on the first print coming out of the Selphy. This is your origin story post. Shoot it like it matters, because it does.

The moment: Coffee or tea near a window. Early spring morning light hitting sideways. Imperfect is perfect — a little steam, soft shadow, natural grain.

Lens: 40mm f/2, wide open. Get close. Let the background dissolve into blur. Fill the frame with feeling, not just the subject.

Lighting: Face a window. Natural side-light only. No flash. Calgary overcast days are actually ideal — soft, even, cinematic.

Find a slow orchestral or soft piano track with under 5,000 uses. Early-mover sweet spot — the algorithm rewards you for using sounds before they peak.
📈
How to find trending audio before it peaks
In Reels editor → tap music icon → "Trending" → look for upward arrows. Sounds under 5K Reels = early adopter advantage. Also watch TikTok — audio hits Instagram 1–2 weeks later.
70/30 rule: 70% of your posts use trending audio (reach), 30% use ambient/original sound (brand authenticity).
