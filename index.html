◈  Today's Shoot
What to Shoot Today

Today you introduce the whole concept. Show your morning — coffee, quiet light, your camera on the table — and end on the first print coming out of the Selphy. This is your origin story post. Shoot it like it matters.

The moment: Coffee or tea near a window. Early spring morning light hitting sideways. Imperfect is perfect — steam, soft shadow, natural grain.

Lens: 40mm f/2, wide open. Get close. Let the background dissolve into blur.

Lighting: Face a window. Natural side-light only. No flash. Calgary overcast days are ideal — soft, even, cinematic.

How to find trending audio before it peaks
Reels editor → music icon → "Trending" → upward arrows. Under 5K Reels = early adopter window. Watch TikTok — audio hits Instagram 1–2 weeks later.
70/30 rule: 70% trending audio (reach) · 30% ambient/original sound (brand). Both matter.
